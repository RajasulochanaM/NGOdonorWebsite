AboutUs: out CCH: Cheshire Homes India, Chennai (CCH) has completed 62 years of distinguished service in the cause of Differently-abled Persons (referred to as ‘Persons with Disabilities’ or PWDs) and children with mental retardation, by providing shelter, food, clothing, medical help, mobility appliances, counseling and care, vocational training, new avenues of employment and rehabilitation. Some of our activities are as below: Providing residential care to 35 differently-abled persons (20 girls and 15 boys) Supporting Community Based Rehabilitation (CBR) Running a Livelihood Resource Centre (LRC) offering vocational training and helping the PWDs get placement in new avenues of employment and rehabilitation Running a Day Care Centre for mentally challenged children (12-18 years) that screens and assesses them and improves their skills through play and pool therapy. The centre trains them in vocational activities, aiming at making them independent. It also helps the parents by free them.
