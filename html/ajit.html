Ajit Foundation

TrustName: Ajit Foundation
Address: Shaha Colony Near Sai Baba Mandir, Talegaon Dabhade, India, Maharashtra
AboutUs: Ajit Foundation is a registered, child-centred, Non-Profit Organization. It is currently working in the districts of Solapur, Osmanabad and Pune, in Maharashtra. The projects include a series of effective and innovative programmes for enhancing the confidence of children so that they can have access to their basic rights. The Foundation especially focuses on the issues related to the girls, who face discrimination in everyday life. Ajit Foundation rightly focuses on bringing changes to the lives of underprivileged children, including those living on the streets, Migrants living in the rural and urban areas, those with disabilities or affected by HIV, also for those who are exploited and trafficked and even the child labourers and children of sex workers.
Trustee: Mahesh Nimbalkar
